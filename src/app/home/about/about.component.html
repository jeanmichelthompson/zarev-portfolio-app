About Me

Born in Colorado but raised abroad in North Macedonia, I spent most of my childhood bouncing between the Rockies and the Balkans. With the distances my youth took me across, I developed a deep appreciation for the world as a whole, and I aim to capture, chronicle, and encapsulate my experience through writing. This portfolio is an example of some of my most recent written work. There is nothing that I find uninteresting or unworthy of writing about.
